The FIFA App

Take FIFA and the best of world football with you everywhere! Live-Score updates for 102 of the world’s top leagues, relive all the action from the FIFA World Cup in Brazil, get world football's latest headlines, photos and videos; the latest FIFA/Coca-Cola World Ranking and more.

iPhone iPad Android

 

The FIFA 1904 App

The FIFA 1904, FIFA’s free football magazine, is available every month both as a paper edition and as an intuitive e-Magazine on your device. It offers fans a diverse blend of the most up-to-date news from FIFA and across the world of football, including fascinating reports on the biggest stars, in-depth analysis, interviews, photos, commentaries, debates and much more.
